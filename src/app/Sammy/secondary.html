I am going into my fourth year of Engineering Science at UofT. My thesis project is to write scalable versions of Shore's and Grovers Quantum algorithms. Because teaching is the best way to learn, I've decided to write guides/blogs on various ways I've gotten ready to do this. Python is a big part of Quantum Algorithms as a majority of the Quantum SDK's (IMBQ and Rigetti Forest) make use of Python. Obviously, I also needed to have a solid grounding in QM. This is why I chose these two sections.
